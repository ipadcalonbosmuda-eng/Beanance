Beanance loves...

Beanance loves to frolic on the beach, where he spends hours playing in the sand, chasing waves, and basking under the sun. He has a knack for building the most imaginative sandcastles and is often found rolling around in the sand to leave behind a series of cute, blob-shaped impressions. Beanance also enjoys collecting shiny pebbles, which he keeps in a secret spot only he knows about. When not at the beach, he loves to bounce around fields, picking flowers and chasing butterflies.
